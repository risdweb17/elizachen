Super Simplified Cheesecake

HERE IT IS

tarte portion:

  • egg yolk — 1
  • sugar — 40g
  • flour — 120g
  1. preheat oven to 350 F
  2. put in food processor
  3. in a cake pan, press the mix tocreate a crust bake at 350 F for 10 min

filling portion:

  • heavy cream — 1 cup
  • 2 eggs
  • cream cheese — 1 pack
  • flour — 3 TBSP
  • sugar — 2/3 cup
  • lemon juice — 1TBSP
  1. put in food processor
  2. pour onto crust
  3. bake at 350 F for about 30 min. time will depend on oven strength.
